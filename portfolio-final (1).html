Case Studies
01
Brookdale Community College · Education
Spring Enrollment Acquisition Campaign
Meta Google SEM Programmatic
+
Objective

Drive qualified leads for spring semester enrollment. Balance top-funnel awareness with bottom-funnel conversion across a competitive education landscape with high CPL.

Strategy
  • Full-funnel architecture: broad interest targeting at top, retargeting at bottom
  • Cross-channel integration — Meta for demand gen, Display for scale, Search for high-intent capture
  • Lookalike audiences built from site visitors with 7–30 day retargeting windows
  • Creative refresh every 2–3 weeks based on CTR and engagement data
  • Tested program-specific vs. career-focused messaging angles
Results
~25%
CPL reduction via audience refinement
~20%
CTR lift through creative testing
↑CVR
Improved via retargeting vs. prospecting
Key Takeaway

Full-funnel strategy is critical in education. Retargeting + first-party data significantly outperforms cold audience prospecting when CPL pressure is high.
